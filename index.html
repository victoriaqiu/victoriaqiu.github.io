Research

  • Skeleton Body Pose Prediction Based On First Person Videos
    07. 2017 – Present
    General Robotics, Automation, Sensing and Perception (GRASP) Laboratory, University of Pennsylvania
    Research Assistant to Prof. Jianbo Shi
    We three-dimensional-reconstructed context from highly jittery, blurry and narrow ego-centric frames with Multi-View Stereo and concatenated a joint-tracking CNN with LSTM to estimate and predict skeleton body pose of camera-holder. We utilized temporal third-person information captured from team members and experimented with ego-centric videos of real cases, like cooking and basketball, showing effectiveness of this model to structure skeleton body pose of camera-holder in three-dimensional context.





  • Hardness Prediction for Object Detection Inspired by Human Vision
    06. 2016 – 01. 2017
    3D Image Simulation Laboratory, Tsinghua University
    Research Assistant to Prof. Huimin Ma
    Introducing human factor into object detection to predict the detection hardness, we fused novel eye tracking features with feature maps in CNN to utilize complicated human visual traits. We further proposed an unsupervised learning approach to generate eye tracking features directly from input images to replace laborious eye tracking experiments.
    This work has been contributed to a first-authored paper, accepted as oral presentation in ICIG 2017.





  • Characterizing Psychological Problems via Interactive Devices
    09. 2017 – Present
    3D Image Simulation Laboratory, Tsinghua University
    Research Assistant to Prof. Huimin Ma
    The aim was to recognize patterns of mental diseases, in behavioral and biometric data from interactive devices. Now we are analyzing medical and behavioral data collected from psychology experiments and diseases institutes and experimenting ML methods.





  • End-to-End Printed Chinese Text Recognition
    12. 2016 – 06. 2017
    Intellectual Graphs and Texts Processing Laboratory, Tsinghua University
    Research Assistant to Prof. Shengjin Wang
    Regarding no appropriate Chinese database is released online, we first constructed THU Chinese printed character database (THU Chinese Database) containing 3500+ categories of Chinese characters for both offline training and validation. We trained a multi-pathway convolutional neural network for character recognition utilizing spatial information and logogram usage in Chinese and made use of semantic information through a statistic Conditional Random Field model boosting accuracy by 3%. This framework achieved a precision of 96.8% on CMCC Chinese Dataset.
    This proposed solution was purchased by China Mobile for product improvement.





  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Registration
    10. 2015 – 12. 2015
    Electrical Engineerign, Stanford University
    Participants in a remote project of Prof. Tsachy Weissman
    I experimented with Maximum Likelihood Estimation approach, a mutual information based registration method, and applied a bias-corrected version of MLE estimator in smooth regime, reducing the Mean Square Error to 1% of traditional MLE approach.
    I completed a technique report and demo, ranking 4th in 146 participants awarded by Outstanding Research Assistant hornor.
